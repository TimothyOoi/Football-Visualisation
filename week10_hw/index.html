Visualisation on the Big 5 European Football Leagues (2021/2022 Season)

The Premier League, Spanish La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1 are among the most entertaining football leagues not only in Europe, but also the world. This visualisation explores these five European football leagues over the 2021/2022 season.

Nationalities of Players

Majority of footballers in the five leagues are European. South America and Africa are also well represented.

Clinical, or simply Lucky?

Expected Goals (xG) measures the likelihood that a player scores from a particular situation during a game. The value of xG ranges from zero to one, where zero represents a chance which is impossible to score. Conversely, a value of one means that a player is expected to score every single time. By analysing the xG and goals scored for a player, we can understand the shooting ability of luck of a particular player. A player who consistently scores more goals than their total xG probably has an above average shooting/finishing ability. Over the 2021/2022 season, Robert Lewandowski was the top scorer in the big 5 European football leagues, scoring 35 league goals across the campaign.
